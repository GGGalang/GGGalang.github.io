CRIME



CRIME, a constant happening in our world. One cannot deny the evil that exists in this world that we live in. As sad as the reality is, it happens every day.This section is designed to report some of the happenings in our country all related to crime in order to make you, the viewer, be aware of recent events. This section will be primarily focused on national crimes, but international crimes will also be covered.

Credits: Al Jazeera

Journalist Known for Investigating Drug War Killed

December 9, 2021

Journalist Jesus Malabanan, known for helping Reuters, an international news agency, in the investigation of Duterte's War against Drugs, has been shot dead. Police stated that he was killed inside his own home while watching television. Further reports state that it was most likely due to a disagreement regarding land (Al Jazeera, 2021).

Credits: Wikipedia Map

Cops Seize P2.7Million Worth of Shabu in Makati City Buy Bust

December 18, 2021

In a recent buy-bust operation created by Makati operatives, Baby Madonna Vivero and Maria Sandra Esponilla were arrested at around 10:30PM on a Thursday. Money and drugs were confiscated by the polics, with 400grams of suspected shabu removed (Inquirer.net, 2021).

Credits: Yamaha

Motorcycle Riding Robbers Shoot Guard Dead in Caloocan

December 15, 2021

In recent events, a 31-year-old security guard was shot dead by two motorcycle-riding men who robbed a delivery company on December 13 in Caloocan. The suspects left a box of coconut husks (Manila Bulletin, 2021).

Credits: Rappler

Police Wait on Lab Results Regarding the Murder of Siblings

December 15, 2021

Teenage siblings were recently murdered in Cotabato on a Wednesday, on December 15. The police launched a probe and are now waiting for lab results before launching a complaint. Things such as hair strands and weapons were sent to the lab to be studied (Rappler, 2021).
